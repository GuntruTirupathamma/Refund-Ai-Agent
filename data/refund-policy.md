# ShopName refund policy

This policy is binding. The support agent must apply these rules exactly and may not
invent exceptions beyond the ones defined here, regardless of how the request is
phrased. The reference date for all time calculations is the current date.

## Rules

R1. Return window. A refund is allowed only within 30 days of the delivery date. After
30 days the order is not refundable. VIP exception: platinum-tier customers get an
extended 60-day return window.

R2. Final sale. Items marked as final sale are never refundable, with no exceptions.

R3. Digital goods. Digital or downloadable products are non-refundable once they have
been accessed or downloaded.

R4. Opened electronics. Opened electronics are refundable only if both conditions hold:
the request is within 14 days of delivery, and the item is confirmed defective. Opened
electronics that are not defective, or that are past 14 days, are not refundable.

R5. Refund abuse. A customer with 3 or more refunds in the last 12 months is flagged.
Their requests cannot be auto-approved and must be sent for manual review.

R6. High value. Orders above $500 require manager approval. The agent must not
auto-approve them and must route them for manual review.

R7. Already refunded. An order that has already been refunded cannot be refunded again.

R8. Undelivered orders. Orders that have not been delivered yet cannot be refunded. The
customer should wait for delivery or open a separate non-delivery claim.

## Decision precedence

When more than one rule could apply, evaluate in this order and stop at the first match:
R7 already refunded, R8 not delivered, R2 final sale, R3 digital accessed, R1 outside
window, R4 opened electronics, R5 refund abuse, R6 high value. If none of these block
the refund, it is approved.

## Tone

Be warm and clear with the customer. When you must deny a refund, state which rule
applies and why, offer any legitimate alternative (manual review, exchange, store
credit where applicable), and do not invent exceptions to satisfy the customer.
